## WHAT IS SATHIO?

**Sathio** is India's voice-first, multilingual AI assistant designed to bridge the digital divide for 500M+ non-English speakers.

> **One-Line Definition:**  
> *A calm, friendly AI that helps Indians complete essential digital tasks — in their own language, without typing.*

### The Name "Sathio"

- **Meaning:** Derived from "Saathi" (साथी) — companion, friend, helper
- **Personality:** The friendly cousin who helps with confusing paperwork
- **Tagline:** "Main hoon na" (I'm here for you)

---

## THE PROBLEM WE SOLVE

### India's Digital Divide

| Reality | Impact |
|---------|--------|
| 22 official languages, 700+ dialects | Digital services mostly in English/Hindi |
| 200-300M citizens locked out | Cannot access banking, government schemes |
| ₹200+ per visit to cyber cafes | Poor families pay 5-10% of monthly income |
| Low digital literacy | Fear of making mistakes online |
| Complex government portals | Citizens miss benefits they're entitled to |

### Current Solutions (And Why They Fail)

| Solution | Problem |
|----------|---------|
| **Google/Alexa/Siri** | Limited Indian languages, no local context |
| **Government portals** | English-first, text-heavy, overwhelming UX |
| **Cyber cafes** | Expensive, unreliable, privacy concerns |
| **Existing apps** | Fragmented, poor voice, no human fallback |

### Our Insight

> **"Talking is always free. We charge for outcomes, not conversation."**

People will talk for hours, but fear clicking buttons. Voice removes barriers — literacy, complexity, fear.

## CORE PHILOSOPHY

### Design Principles

| Principle | What It Means |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Voice First** | Big mic button, minimal typing, spoken responses |
| **Bharat First** | Built for India's languages, network conditions, devices |
| **Outcome, Not Answers** | Success = completed task, not just information |
| **Trust Through Calm** | No urgency, no jargon, patient guidance |
| **Human When Needed** | AI fallback to humans for complex cases |

### Brand Personality

Sathio speaks like a **helpful older cousin** — not a teacher, not a robot, not a salesperson.

| Trait | How It Shows |
|-------|--------------|
| **Friendly** | "Main hoon na" — I'm here for you |
| **Patient** | Never rushes, allows pauses, repeats happily |
| **Respectful** | Uses honorifics (ji, aap), no judgment |
| **Calm** | Steady tone, no urgency, no exclamations |
| **Encouraging** | "Ho jaayega" — it'll be okay |

### Voice Design

- **Gender:** Neutral (culturally safer across India)
- **Speed:** Medium-slow (~140 words/minute)
- **Tone:** Warm, reassuring, clear
- **Accent:** Simple pan-Indian (no regional bias)

### Script Examples

| Situation | Sathio Says |
|-----------|-------------|
| First greeting | "Namaste! Main Sathio hoon. Batao, kya madad chahiye?" |
| Returning user | "Welcome back! Aaj kya poochhna hai?" |
| Processing | "Ek second... samajh raha hoon." |
| Confusion | "Thoda clear bol doge?" |
| Network issue | "Lagta hai network thoda slow hai." |
| Task complete | "Ho gaya! Badhai ho!" |
| Ending | "Aur kuch madad chahiye?" → "Theek hai. Jab bhi zarurat ho, bula lena." |

### Banned Words

Never use: "Error", "Invalid", "Failed", "System", "Processing", "AI model", technical jargon, or urgent/salesy language.

## WHO WE SERVE

### Primary Users

| Persona | Demographics | Primary Use |
|---------|--------------|-------------|
| **Ramesh (Farmer)** | 35-60, rural UP, Hindi | PM-Kisan, Aadhaar, mandi prices |
| **Lakshmi (Shopkeeper)** | 30-50, semi-urban TN, Tamil | GST, loans, UPI |
| **Sharma Ji (Senior)** | 60+, Tier-2 MP, Hindi | Pension, healthcare |
| **Priya (Urban Youth)** | 15-30, migrant Karnataka | Family assistance, scholarships |
| **Abdul (Migrant Worker)** | 25-40, Mumbai from WB, Bengali | Remittances, ration card |

### User Characteristics

- **Device:** Basic Android (2-3 years old, 2GB RAM)
- **Network:** Slow 3G/4G, frequent disconnections
- **Literacy:** Class 8 education typical
- **Language:** Strong regional preference
- **Trust:** Built through patience and success

## WHAT WE DO (MVP)

### Core Capabilities

1. **Voice-First Interface**
   - Tap-to-talk, minimal typing
   - Spoken responses in user's language
   - Works on 2G networks

2. **4 Languages (MVP)**
   - Hindi, Bengali, Tamil, Marathi
   - Code-mixing support (Hindi+English)

3. **Government Services**
   - Aadhaar download/update
   - PAN card application
   - PM-Kisan registration
   - Pension information
   - Ration card guidance

4. **Utility Payments (Guidance)**
   - Electricity bills
   - Mobile recharge
   - Gas booking

5. **Health & Education Info**
   - Ayushman Bharat eligibility
   - Scholarship information
   - Emergency helplines

6. **Step-by-Step Guided Mode**
   - Progressive disclosure
   - Voice + visual instructions
   - Pause and resume

7. **Human Escalation**
   - "Talk to Human" option
   - Expert consultations (paid)
   - WhatsApp support

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

**Q: Why voice-first?**  
A: 200M+ Indians are uncomfortable with text. Voice removes literacy barriers.

**Q: Why not just make better government portals?**  
A: We're not a portal. We're a guide that helps navigate ANY portal in the user's language.

**Q: How do we handle misinformation?**  
A: All responses verified against official sources. Low-confidence answers escalate to humans.

**Q: Why these 4 languages first?**  
A: Hindi (550M), Bengali (265M), Tamil (80M), Marathi (90M) = ~1B speakers combined.

**Q: What about feature phones?**  
A: IVR system planned for Phase 3 to reach the remaining 200M.

**Q: How do we compete with Google?**  
A: They optimize for global. We optimize for Ramesh in rural UP. Different problem.
